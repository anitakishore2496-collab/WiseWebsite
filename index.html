About the Toolkit

The Traffic Impact Decision Toolkit is designed for transportation engineers, urban planners, and consultants who need to make **fast, defensible, and high-confidence decisions** on development projects. Avoid over-scoping studies, reduce reviewer challenges, and deliver clear recommendations every time.

Toolkit Features

Scope Decision Framework

Determine when a Traffic Impact Analysis is required and how to justify the appropriate scope for any project.

Trip Generation & Assumptions

Templates and guidance for realistic trip generation, adjusting assumptions, and sensitivity testing for defensible results.

LOS & Mitigation Decisions

Frameworks to balance Level of Service (LOS), safety, reliability, and cost-effectiveness in mitigation strategies.

Executive Summary Templates

Client-ready summary templates and communication tools to justify decisions and respond to reviewers efficiently.

Decision Trees & Checklists

Step-by-step decision trees and checklists to systematically evaluate every traffic and development scenario.

Excel & Word Tools

Ready-to-use spreadsheets and report templates to streamline your workflow and save hours on each project.
